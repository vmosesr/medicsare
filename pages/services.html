Our Specialties

Advanced Cardiac Care

Our cardiology department provides comprehensive diagnosis and treatment of heart conditions using the latest technology and evidence-based approaches.

  • Non-invasive cardiac testing
  • Cardiac catheterization
  • Electrophysiology studies
  • Pacemaker implantation
  • Cardiac rehabilitation programs

Comprehensive Orthopedic Services

Our orthopedic specialists diagnose and treat conditions affecting the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles.

  • Joint replacement surgery
  • Sports medicine
  • Spine surgery
  • Fracture care
  • Physical therapy programs

Comprehensive Cancer Care

Our oncology department offers personalized cancer treatment plans using the latest advances in cancer care to provide the best possible outcomes for our patients.

  • Medical oncology
  • Radiation oncology
  • Surgical oncology
  • Hematology services
  • Cancer support services

Advanced Neurological Care

Our neurology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves.

  • Neurological diagnostics
  • Epilepsy monitoring
  • Stroke care and rehabilitation
  • Movement disorders treatment
  • Headache and pain management
